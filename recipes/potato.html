Potatoes

Description

Potatoes comes from the ground and can be eaten in any way, shape or form

Ingredients

  • Any type of potatoes
  • Any type of Seasoning

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil over high heat
  2. Pour the potatoes into the water
  3. Reduce heat to medium
  4. Pour the seasoning into the potatoes
  5. Cook at rapid simmer until potatoes are easily pierced with a paring knife or a fork(10 minutes for chopped potatoes and 20 minutes for whole potatoes)
  6. Drain potatoes in colander
  7. Serve
