Description

Sausages are sort of thin parboiled sausages that contain a lot of water and are traditionally made of pork, beef or horse meat. This food product comes from Germany and it is typical in the northern countries of Europe.

Ingredients

  • Any type of sausage
  • Any type of bun
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Onion (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the Sausage for 8-10 minutes at medium-high heat.
  2. Prep the Buns and Toppings of choice
  3. Grab a Pinch or a fork
  4. Grab a Sausage with the Pinch or Fork
  5. Lay the Sausage on the Bun and add the Toppings of your choice, and Enjoy!
