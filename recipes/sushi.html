Sushi

Description

Sushi is typically made with rice, nori (seaweed), fish, and vegetables. Common sushi ingredients include tuna, salmon, shrimp, squid, eel, and crab.

Ingredients

  • rice
  • nori (seeweed)
  • fish
  • vegetables

Steps

  1. Prep the Rice & Nori. Lay out the nori on the bamboo mat (or whatever you're using)
  2. Spread the Rice on the Nori
  3. Add Fillings to the Bottom Quarter of the Nori
  4. Roll the Sushi Part Way
  5. Roll the Sushi the Rest of the Way
  6. Move to the Cutting Board
  7. Cut the Sushi
  8. Serve
